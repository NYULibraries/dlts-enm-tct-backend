Artists I Love

Matt Nishi-Broach

Joseph Cornell

Surrealist sculptor and collagist. Fantastic diarama artist

James Turrell

California Light and Space artist. Made my favorite artwork in NYC ('Meeting', at PS1)

Francisco de Goya y Luciente

Spanish painter and printmaker. Subtly parodied the royalty as a court painter. Documented the horrors of the Napoleonic Wars in his etchings. Found the darker side of human Life.
